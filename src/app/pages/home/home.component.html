About Us

Established in 2003, Mentee Medical College has been providing the best in training and education to members of the Lawrencville, Georgia community. The programs we offer provide career-focused medical training that is dedicated to teaching students how to handle real world situations, giving future medical professionals the tools and confidence they will need to be the caretakers of tomorrow.

Nursing Assistant

Start your journey in the medical field. Our nursing courses will teach you how to be the best in an ever growing field, while also preparing you for your future courses at Mentee College.

Ultrasound Program
