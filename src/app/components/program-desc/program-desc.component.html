Nursing Assistant Program Objective

The objective of our Nursing Assistant Program is to prepare students with the knowledge, skills and abilities essential for the provision of basic care to residents / patients in health care settings. The program consists of various units of instruction, laboratory experience, lecture, clinical / externship. Upon satisfactory completion of training, students may register with the State, and be qualified to assume entry level positions in a variety of health care settings.
